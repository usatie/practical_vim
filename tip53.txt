# Delete Around, or Change Inside

iw    : word
iW    : WORD
is    : sentence
ip    : paragraph
aw    : word plus space(s)
aW    : WORD plus space(s)
as    : sentence plus space(s)
ap    : paragraph plus blank line(s)

Improve your writing by deleting excellent adjectives.

## General Tips
