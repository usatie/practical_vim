# Use Alternate Spelling Dictionaries
:h 'spelllang'

## Specify a Regional Variant of a Language
moustache is British spelling.
mustache is American spelling.

:set spell
:set spelllang=en_s

## Obtain Spell Files for Other Languages
http://ftp.vim.org/vim/runtime/spell/
:set spelllang=fr
:h spellfile.vim
